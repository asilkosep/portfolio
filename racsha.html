Racsha

Water conservation campaign website

A competition to rethink access to clean drinking water.

I entered a competition called Water Futures, which was aimed at educating and making a change in the way that we consume water in urban spaces, and otherwise. I worked on this project with a partner and truly enjoyed what we came up with, however I did this before I had studied the principles of UI/UX design. I decided to revisit the project with my newly gained perspective on digital design and see if I could make it stronger and more viable as a product. Below you can see a video of the original campaign website design.
